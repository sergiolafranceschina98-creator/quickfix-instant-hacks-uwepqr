✅ Sign Up Flow

   - Sign in with demo credentials above

✅ Problem Solving
   - Select category (cleaning, tech, finance, etc.)
   - Enter problem description
   - Verify AI solution appears within 10 seconds

✅ Image Analysis
   - Take photo with camera
   - Verify image uploads successfully
   - Check that AI analyzes image and provides solution

✅ Save Hacks
   - Get a solution
   - Tap "Save This Hack"
   - Verify success modal appears
   - Check hack appears in Saved tab

✅ Delete Hacks
   - Go to Saved tab
   - Tap trash icon on a hack
   - Confirm deletion in modal
   - Verify hack is removed from list

✅ Sign Out
   - Tap logout button in Saved tab
   - Confirm sign out in modal
   - Verify redirect to auth screen

✅ Session Persistence
   - Sign in
   - Close app completely
   - Reopen app
   - Verify still logged in
